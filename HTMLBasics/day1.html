BUILDING BLOCK OF LIFE : THE CELL

WHAT IS CELL?

A cell is the basic building block of all living organisms.

TYPES OF CELL

There are two primary typesâ€”prokaryotic cells (like bacteria) and eukaryotic cells (which make up plants, animals, and fungi).
Eukaryotic cells are more complex and have a defined nucleus.

STRUCTURE OF CELL

A typical eukaryotic cell has a nucleus containing genetic material (DNA), mitochondria for energy production, a cell membrane that controls what goes in and out, and various other organelles that perform specific functions.

FUNCTIONS OF CELL

Cells perform all the essential functions for life, such as energy production, waste elimination, and replication.

SPECIALIZATION OF CELL

In multicellular organisms, cells become specialized to perform specific tasks.
For example, nerve cells (neurons) transmit signals, while muscle cells contract to allow movement.
